My travel photos

Ukraine ❤️

Ukraine has a variety of tourist attractions to offer, such as festivals honouring its vibrant culture and a lovely collection of historic buildings. Ukrainians are some of the friendliest people in Europe. From the undulating countryside to the cobblestone alleyways of old city centres the country absolutely gorgeous.

Iceland

Iceland, the land of fire and ice, has become a popular travel destination over the past few years. From the country’s magnificent glaciers, thundering waterfalls and epic natural nighttime displays, it’s easy to see why travellers are escaping to the edge of the world.

Greese

Greece is known as the cradle of Western Civilisation and the birthplace of democracy, theatre, the Olympic Games, and much more. The country is famed for its ancient history and ruins, incredible culture and food, and gorgeous island landscapes. Greece is home to up to 6,000 islands and islets.

Italy

With some of the world’s most impressive art, architecture, food and scenery, it’s little wonder that Italy tops so many traveller’s bucket lists. From ancient cities and soaring mountains to fine wines and traditional pizza, you could explore this fascinating country for years and still not discover everything.

Turkey

No other country sits at the confluence of cultures like Turkey, the land bridge between vast continents, kingly empires, and eras of world history. There is no signature scent, flavor, style, or experience to be had — here, your sense of time and place gets turned on its head.
